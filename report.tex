		In preparation for runs at AWAKE in CERN simulations of the beam and
		measurement of beam parameters with a spectrometer were carried out in
		order to investigate how the spectrometer behaves under changes to
		experimental parameters. The energy spreads of the beam were tested and
		a percentage energy spread of \SI{4}{\percent} is the cutoff point at
		which measurements become reliable. Emittance values were tested and
		amittances above \num{e-5}{\meter\radian} can be taken to be inaccurate.
